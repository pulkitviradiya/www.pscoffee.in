Survey Disclosure Notice.

Your responses are helping shape the concept before it exists.

This notice applies to location votes, menu feedback, subscription-interest forms, waitlists, vendor outreach, and other research flows on pscoffee.in.

Read the notice

Why we ask.

The point is not to collect noise. The point is to learn what should actually be built, where, and for whom.

1. Purpose of surveys and feedback.

P.S. Coffee uses surveys, votes, forms, and feedback tools to evaluate the viability of the concept, understand potential customer demand, shortlist Pod locations, refine the menu, assess subscription interest, identify potential vendors and roasters, and improve the eventual product direction.

2. Voluntary participation.

Your participation in any survey, waitlist, or feedback form is voluntary. You may choose not to respond or not to complete a submission.

3. What may be collected.

We may collect survey responses, votes, location preferences, contact details, business details, qualitative comments, usage intent, product preferences, and other information you voluntarily provide. We may also associate this with broad analytics data about how the form or page was used.

4. How responses may be used.

Responses may be reviewed individually or in aggregate for concept validation, launch planning, location prioritisation, pricing review, menu refinement, partner screening, and strategic decision-making. We may use anonymised or aggregated learnings internally in decks, planning materials, or research summaries.

5. No guaranteed outcome.

Your response helps inform decisions, but it does not guarantee that a Pod will open in a requested location, that a menu item will launch, or that any specific feature, price, app workflow, or subscription plan will be adopted.

6. Concept-stage disclosure.

P.S. Coffee is still in concept stage. Surveys are being used before launch to understand whether and how the project should move forward. This is research activity, not confirmation that the final business model has been fixed.

7. Contact and follow-up.

If you provide contact details, we may follow up regarding your submission, ask clarifying questions, share relevant concept updates, or notify you when the project becomes live.

8. Data handling.

Survey data is handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy. We aim to use collected information for internal survey and research purposes and related launch communications, rather than for unrelated or deceptive marketing activity.

9. Contact.

Questions about survey participation or data use may be sent to hello@pscoffee.in.
